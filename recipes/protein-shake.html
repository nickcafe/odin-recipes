Protein Shake!

Yummy protein shake to help you hit your protein goals!

This creamy high-protein shake will keep you satisfied for hours and tastes like a chocolate-peanut butter banana milkshake. You don't even need to add protein powder, thanks to the naturally occurring protein in the soymilk, Greek yogurt and peanut butter.

Ingredients

  • Protein Powder
  • Nuts
  • Fruits
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Maple Syrup

Steps

  1. Add all of the ingredients into a high-speed blender.
  2. Blend on high for 1 minute, stopping to scrape the sides, if needed. Add more almond milk to thin things out as needed.
  3. Enjoy!
